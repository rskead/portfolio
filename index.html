about

Hello! My name is Rose-sharon, however I go by just Rose. I'm a proud First Nations Indigenous woman who has a passion for design & technology. I love learning, exploring, and creating! I was born in Red Lake, my community is Mishkeegogamang First Nation, and my current home is Thunder Bay, ON.

In spring 2020, I will be graduating from Confederation College's Interactive Media Development (IMD) program. I am a graphic, UI, & interactive media designer, however I like to learn about and utilize other subjects such as photography, illustration, animation, & creative coding.

self-portrait

Software: Adobe Illustrator

This is my self-portrait I created in first year. Instead of realism or my usual cartoon-y look, I chose to do it in a low-poly style to show a different kind of depth.

save tbay pools

Software: Adobe Illustrator, Photoshop, XD

Save Tbay Pools is a fictional campaign based on real events in Thunder Bay, Ontario. Thunder Bay was getting ready to close down Dease Pool, a place that was important in the city's history. For my poster & app ui, my concept was inclusion and fun for everyone!

GPA CALC

Software: Adobe Illustrator

Coding Language: NativeScript, JavaScript

GPA CALC is the first app I designed and programmed myself. It is a rough-prototype app for students to calculate and save their GPA calculations.

Everyday Ojibway

Software: Adobe Illustrator, XD

Everyday Ojibway is an app I designed the UI for. It is an ojibway language learning app for young adults that want to learn conversational ojibway.
