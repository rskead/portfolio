About

My name is Rose Skead and I am a proud First Nations Indigenous student attending Confederation College. I was born in Red Lake, however my community is Mishkeegogamang First Nation, and my current home is Thunder Bay.

I am currently enrolled in the Interactive Media Development program and will be graduating in 2020. Ever since I can remember, design and technology has been my passion. I've always been creative, fast-learning, & hard-working.
